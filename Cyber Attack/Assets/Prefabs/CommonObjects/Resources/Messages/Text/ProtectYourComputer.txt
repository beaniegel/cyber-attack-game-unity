<size=24><b>Protect Your Computer</b></size>

By connecting your computer to the unprotected internet connections (e.g. public wifi), downloading files, installing programs from untrusted sources you provide opportunities for dishonest people to hack your personal device.

Pick and install appropriate tools to defend your computer and defeat the hackers <i>before</i> they reach the core of your device and <i>cause you damage!</i>
